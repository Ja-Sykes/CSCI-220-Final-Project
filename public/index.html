What is Rocket League?

Boost, Fly, and Score: How Rocket League Works

Car Soccer Explained:

Rocket League is a wildly popular video game that is often described as "soccer with rocket-powered cars." The core concept is simple: two teams of players, each controlling a small, extremely fast car, compete in an arena to hit a giant soccer ball into the opposing team's oversized goal.

What makes it unique is the combination of traditional sports rules with acrobatic vehicular chaos. Your car can jump, flip, and, most importantly, activate a rocket boost that allows you to fly across the field and even hit the ball mid-air. This mix of driving, boosting, and flying creates moments of intense, high-speed action and requires surprising skill, teamwork, and physics knowledge to master. It’s an easy game to pick up and play for a quick laugh, but a difficult one to master, which is why it has grown into a major international esport.

The USF Fort Wayne Cougars: Competing in the NECC

The University of Saint Francis (USF) in Fort Wayne, Indiana, has established a robust Esports program that recognizes the rapid growth of competitive gaming, with Rocket League serving as one of its premiere titles. USF offers student-athletes the unique opportunity to combine their passion for high-level vehicular soccer with their academic pursuits. Our dedicated facilities provide a sophisticated environment where the Cougars hone the complex maneuvers—from high-flying aerial passes to calculated goalie saves—required to excel at the top collegiate level.

The USF Rocket League team focuses its competitive efforts on the NECC (National Esports Collegiate 4 Conference), one of the leading organizations for college esports. Competing in the NECC means the Cougars regularly face highly skilled opponents from across the nation, providing intense, structured competition. This platform not only challenges our players to execute perfect strategy and teamwork but also provides national visibility, ensuring the University of Saint Francis remains at the forefront of the collegiate esports landscape as they battle for conference supremacy.
